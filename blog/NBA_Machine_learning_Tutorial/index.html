What are we doing?

I’m going to show you the steps that I took in order to clean, structure, and run the data used to predict a player's PER rating with an accuracy of at least 95%.

If you have no idea what the PER score is used for, let me explain. PER(Player Efficiency Rating), developed by John Hollinger, is a number used by the NBA to rate a player's performance throughout the season. If you scroll down to the bottom of the wiki page, you'll see Hollinger's original formula. Don't worry, we're not going to touch on that at all, actually we're really not going to do much math.
